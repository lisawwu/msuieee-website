EVENTS

Connecting With Professors

Here at MSU, we have many faculty members who are IEEE members themselves. We provide you with opportunities to learn about their cutting edge research conducted right here on campus directly from the leading researcher through our professor talk series.

Whether you want to get into research or build connections with our faculty researchers, take advantage of the academic IEEE network.

Technical Workshops

Our MSU IEEE Student Branch hosts a variety of hands-on technical workshops designed to enhance your skills and prepare you for real-world applications. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced engineer, our workshops provide valuable learning opportunities in a collaborative environment.

Intro to PCB Design

Learn the fundamentals of printed circuit board (PCB) design using industry-standard software and create your own custom circuits.

Embedded Systems & Microcontrollers

Get hands-on experience programming microcontrollers like Arduino and STM32 for real-world applications.

Soldering & Hardware Prototyping

Master soldering techniques and assemble your own electronic circuits from scratch.

Seminars and Professional Tours

At MSU IEEE Student Branch, we provide opportunities for students to connect with industry professionals, gain career insights, and explore cutting-edge technology through our seminars and professional tours.

These events are a great way to learn, network, and gain a competitive edge in your career. Join us to take advantage of these valuable experiences!

Industry Speaker Series

Hear from experienced engineers, researchers, and industry leaders about the latest trends in technology, career advice, and innovation.

Professional Company Tours

Visit leading tech companies, engineering firms, and research labs to see real-world applications of engineering and network with professionals.

IEEE & Networking Events

Engage with IEEE professionals, expand your network, and explore mentorship opportunities.
