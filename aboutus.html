INSTITUTE OF ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONICS ENGINEERS (IEEE)

IEEE is the world’s largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. Through its global network, IEEE supports innovation in electrical engineering, computer engineering, electronics, telecommunications, and many related fields.

At Michigan State University, our IEEE Student Branch brings students together through workshops, networking opportunities, technical projects, and community events. We aim to help members build practical skills, explore career paths, and connect with peers who share an interest in engineering and technology.

Our organization welcomes students from all backgrounds who are interested in learning, collaborating, and growing professionally. Whether you are looking for hands-on experience, industry connections, or a supportive engineering community, MSU IEEE offers opportunities to get involved.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to foster technological innovation, professional development, and community among students at Michigan State University. We strive to create opportunities for members to strengthen their technical knowledge, leadership abilities, and connections within the engineering field.

Through events, workshops, and collaborative initiatives, MSU IEEE supports students in preparing for academic success and future careers while promoting the broader values of innovation, service, and excellence in technology.
